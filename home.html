About Me

My name is Katie Green and I began my path in technology the summer of 2023.

I spent my previous years as a successful hairdresser, starting with corporate Great Clips and working my way to booth rental and finally a salon owner.
The transition was nothing short of huge, however, I perservered and have pathed out a new life for myself and I couldn't be more excited!
I am still developing my passion in the coding world by continually learning from different resources and working on several projects.


My outside world and hobbies consist of being a business co-owner to GreenTree Events, where we host events and rent music equipemnt. I also love to DJ, which plays a special role in owning that business.
I also have a passion for being creative in several other ways. I love to craft, cook, draw, and sew. I also am a huge movie buff with my favorites being the creations of Hayao Miyazaki, and you can often catch me playing my favorite game, Zelda, on my Switch.
